Profile

Name: Plasma Mouse

Gender: Male

Age: Unknown

Race: Anthro

.

Plasma Mouse is Stephen's friendly rival.

Appearance

Plasma Mouse is a white antrhopomorphic mouse with black hair, and yellow eyes. He is equipped with a suit of armor consisting of a green shirt, dark grey tights, a brown belt with a golden "PM" emblem, and a device on his back. He also wears black gloves with grey rings, and black boots with green sides and neon blue rings.

Personality

Not much is known about Plasma Mouse's personality. His favorite food is pizza.

Arsenal

Plasma Mouse is capable of manipulating plasma, hence his name. He can also mould and fire plasma, and use it as a weapon.

Plasma Mouse sometimes uses hand-to-hand and magic for combat. He also has a visor that can fire lasers, as well as a helmet that can shoot electricity. He is also capable of transmuting his arm into a cannon.
