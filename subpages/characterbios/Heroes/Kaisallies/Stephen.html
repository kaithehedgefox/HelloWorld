Stephen the Fox Boy

Profile

Name: Stephen

Gender: Male

Age: Unknown

Race: 1/2 Animal 1/2 Human

.

Appearance

Stephen is a fox boy with orange eyes, a black snout, fox ears, a bushy fox tail, fox fangs, and long orange hair tied in a ponytail held by a grey ribbon. He is covered in orange fur, except for the fur on his chest which is white. His hands and feet are paws and are covered in black fur.

Stephen wears a grey tank top, brown pants, a grey belt with an "S" on the golden buckle. He also wears dark grey shoes and brown gloves.

Personality

Stephen doesn't eat many fruits or vegetables often. He primarily eats meat, such as mice, and chicken.

Arsenal

Stephen is capable of manipulating Water, Fire, Air, Earth, and Electricity. Along with Light and Darkness. He is also capable of shapeshifting and teleportation.

Stephen also uses knives, shurikens, and other weapons as well. He often fires, or throws projectiles.
