Kai Kai is the main protagonist of his own series.

Appearance

Kai is a dark grey anthropomorphic hedgefox with large brown eyes, large fox ears, and spiky dark grey hair with blonde bangs that sprouts outward rather than flows down.

In his civilian from, he wears a dark grey t-shirt, dark grey pants, and white shoes and gloves. In his armored form, he is equipped with a grey suit of armor consisting of a white chestplate with a "1" on the front, white boots and white gauntlets.

Personality

Kai loves anything without vegetables. His favorite food is pizza.

Arsenal

Kai's main weapon is a buster, an arm cannon that fires plasma shots. It can be charged up to fire bigger and stronger projectiles. He can transmute either or both of his arms into arm cannons. Kai can also turn his arm into a lightsaber for melee combat. He can also charge it up to fire sword beams.
